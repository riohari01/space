WRKZ

iSpace WrkZ is an Operations Execution & Marketing System (OES), one of the suites of manufacturing systems designed to execute operations tasks such as Production, Maintenance, Supply Chain or Inventory Management. WrkZ has the Digital Business Process Automation capabilities and capacities to address the Business Process issues which is common for the Manufacturing, Services and Process industrial service providers. Using WrkZ, Organisations will be able to carry on the daily operation by creating Business Process (Work Order, Job Order) and by following the Statement of Processes (SoP’s) to communicate and track the work flow throughout the life cycle of the Business Process..

3 type(s) of BP:
- Operational Process
- Support Process
- Management Process

By digitizing the operations using, WrkZ automated Business Process framework, Organisations will be able to Streamline & Automate the Business Process models, reduce paper work and improve in Planning, Monitoring, Tracking of business operations and costing involved.
